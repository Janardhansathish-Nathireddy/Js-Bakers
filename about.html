About Us

India is a home to over 400 million vegetarian people today, and in a country where no occasion is fully celebrated without the goodness of sweets, finding a quality vegetarian bakery is a task. But, no more! Swiss Castle Bakers got you covered veggies!

Swiss Castle Bakers are pioneers in serving pure vegetarian Cakes and Pastries in the city of Nawabs: Hyderabad. We have been serving quality products with the purity of vegetarian for more than two decades now. Leaders in the vegetarian food industry; we specialize in baking customized, attractive looking and deliciously soothing cakes. We also serve pastries, cookies, muffins, snacks and various other bakery products that are filled with freshness of purity and goodness of love.

We began our journey as a small sweet and bakery shop in Ameerpet area and are growing ever since. With customer satisfaction as our top priority and serving the topmost quality as our motto we have now opened nine outlets already and will very soon make our presence felt at the national platform.
